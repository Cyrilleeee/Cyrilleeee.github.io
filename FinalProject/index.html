Biography


Hello there!

I'm Cyrille Candelaria, a high school senior at Union Square Academy for Health Sciences. I am a Filipina born in the Philippines and moved to NYC when I was eight years old. I'll be turning 18 this November.

I am a food enthusiast and love to bake. I also love to travel and explore new places. I am currently learning how to code and I am excited to see where this journey will take me.

Experience

It’s my first tech/coding experience in which I learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and Java Script. I developed this website and my Food Blog as my projects. I am excited to see where this journey will take me.
